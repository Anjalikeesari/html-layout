About us

"Night view" refers to the ability to see in low-light conditions, either through natural human vision or with the aid of technology like night-vision devices. It can also be used to describe a scenic view of a city or landscape at night.

The ability to see in dim light: This is the most common and scientific meaning, also known as night vision or scotopic vision. Natural: The human ability to see when there is reduced light, such as from moonlight. This ability varies between species; for example, humans have poorer night vision compared to cats. Technological: The ability to see in the dark using devices like night-vision goggles or binoculars, which enhance low-light images.
